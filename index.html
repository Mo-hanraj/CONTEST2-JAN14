Why did iron Man have to die? Iron Man’s sacrifice in Avengers: Endgame was the only way to keep the universe safe, but it was also a means to fix his past mistakes. Over time, Iron Man (Robert Downey, Jr.) grew from a self-absorbed billionaire who exuded arrogance into a hero deeply committed to saving everyone. Despite not having superpowers, he had shown exceptional bravery, always being the first one to charge into the unknown and efficiently lead the team into victory. However, Iron Man’s journey towards being a full-fledged hero was not perfect, as along with it came a series of mistakes that were mended by his heroic death.
